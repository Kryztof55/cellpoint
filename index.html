OUR SOLUTIONS
ADAPT TO ANY MODEL

Fully Outsourced

An external party manages the end to end solution. The operator sells the program, collects the premium, obtains a B&C fee and might undertake limited fulfillment activities.

C3llpoint helps to improve overall efficiency of the programm, increasing profits

Self Administered

The operator manages the end to end solution, depending on the legal and compliance they use an underwriter to provide insurance protection. Might outsource none-core activities.

The operator does not have a device protection program and might need to launch one to obtain additional revenue streams, reduce churn and increase competittive advantage.

C3llpoint helps to launch and operate new program from end-to-end
